Meditation Techniques

Explore a variety of meditation techniques designed by Osho to help you find inner peace and mindfulness. Each technique is a unique path to self-discovery and spiritual growth.

Active Meditations

These dynamic meditations involve movement and activity to release pent-up energy and prepare the mind for stillness.

Dynamic Meditation

A powerful technique involving chaotic movement, catharsis, and silent observation.

Gibberish Meditation

A social meditation that uses gibberish and movement to release mental chatter and connect with others.

Whirling Meditation

A heart-centered meditation that combines Sufi whirling with silent sitting.

Passive Meditations

These techniques focus on stillness and observation, allowing you to deepen your awareness and find inner tranquility.

Nadahrahma Meditation

A guided meditation that uses specific breathing patterns to balance the mind and body.

Kundalini Meditation

A meditation that involves lying down and listening to music, allowing the body to absorb the vibrations.

Vipassana Meditation

A simple yet profound technique of observing your breath without controlling it.
